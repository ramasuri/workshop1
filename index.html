  <section id="about">

    <h2>About the Workshop</h2>

    <p>

      SynData@SIGMOD 2026 focuses on synthetic data generation, augmentation, and management —

      including LLM-driven and classical methods — for analytics, model training, and evaluation

      in data-scarce domains.

    </p>

  </section>


  <section id="cfp">

    <h2>Call for Papers</h2>

    <p>

      We invite submissions on all aspects of synthetic data creation and use, including:

    </p>

    <ul>

      <li>Data synthesis for structured, semi-structured, and unstructured data</li>

      <li>LLM-assisted data generation and enrichment</li>

      <li>Privacy-preserving synthetic data and evaluation metrics</li>

      <li>Applications in analytics, ML training, and benchmarking</li>

    </ul>

    <p>

      <strong>Submission deadline:</strong> November 14, 2025, 5 PM PT<br>

      <strong>Workshop date:</strong> June 2026 (in conjunction with SIGMOD 2026)

    </p>
